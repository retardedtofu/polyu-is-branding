The Kaleidoscope

Seventy-two quarter-discs rotating in synchronised 90° turns — by row, by column, by checkerboard, in waves. Patterns emerge and dissolve, but every move is a quarter-turn on the grid: play within rules. An ambient loop for lecture-hall pre-roll and receptions.

One beat every 1.6 s · transition cubic-bezier(0.65, 0, 0.2, 1)
