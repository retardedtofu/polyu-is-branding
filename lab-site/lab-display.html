The Display

Shrink the cells, enlarge the grid, and the system becomes a low-resolution display — every pixel a quarter-disc that aims where the signal flows. Four programmes: a flow field, a marquee in a 5×7 cell font, the monogram super-scaled, and a live camera filter. Camera frames are processed entirely in your browser — nothing is recorded or transmitted.

Programmes auto-cycle · 44 × 16 raster · camera stays local
