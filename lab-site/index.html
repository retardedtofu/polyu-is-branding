The Lab

The system,
in motion

Eight working pieces built on the Cell System — motion studies, production tools and campaign interactives. Each exhibit is its own page, with a palette switcher to compare all four direction colour stories. The vocabulary is now five primitives: quarter, split square, circle, square, ghost.

EXHIBIT 1 · MOTION

The Assembly

The monogram builds itself — clockwork travel along the grid, 90° rotation snaps.
EXHIBIT 2 · MOTION

The Display

The grid as a low-res screen: flow field, cell-font marquee, monogram — and a live camera filter.
EXHIBIT 3 · MOTION

The Kaleidoscope

Seventy-two discs turning in step — ambient patterns for screens and receptions.
EXHIBIT 4 · TOOL

The Composer

Stamp, rotate, export — production SVG/PNG from the system grid. Now with circle and square.
EXHIBIT 5 · TOOL

Your Mark

Hash anything — any language, any length — into a cell mark on any grid up to 256×256. Same input, same mark, forever.
EXHIBIT 6 · CAMPAIGN

The Open Seat

Nineteen taken, eleven breathing — the founding cohort as an invitation.
EXHIBIT 7 · CAMPAIGN

The Toy Bin

Real physics, then discipline: pieces tumble, snap to the grid, release, reset.
EXHIBIT 8 · TOOL

The Cell Code

A scannable mark: data in shape and rotation, brand in colour. Encode, then read it back with the camera.
