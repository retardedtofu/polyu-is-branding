The Composer

Build cell graphics on the grid and export them as SVG or PNG. There are five brushes (quarter, split, circle, square, and erase), five colour families, three canvas sizes, and the named motifs as presets. Click to stamp, click a quarter again to rotate, and right-click the canvas to save or copy it as SVG.
