The Assembly

Every cell travels along the grid: down one axis, around a corner, then home, snapping to 90° turns as it goes. Nothing drifts freely; the motion follows the same grid the layouts use. Use it as a website hero or a title sequence, or play it in reverse for an outro.

Motion spec: Manhattan paths · 90° rotation snaps at each turn · 950 ms / cell · 80 ms stagger · cubic-bezier(0.6, 0, 0.12, 1) per leg · collective settle 240 ms · honours prefers-reduced-motion.
