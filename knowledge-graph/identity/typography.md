# Typography

> ⚠️ No font names are documented in the supplied materials. Identifications below are visual judgements from the brochure and slogan artwork — **confirm exact typefaces/licences with the designer** (the .ai files in the logo pack will contain them).

## Display — thin geometric sans (Futura/Century Gothic family look)

- All-caps, light weight, generous letterspacing, perfectly circular O's, single-storey geometry.
- Used for: the [[tagline]] lockup, brochure cover headline, big page titles ("WHY INTERDISCIPLINARY STUDIES?", "PROGRAMME OVERVIEW") reversed white on gradient panels.
- Personality: modern, precise, architectural — pairs with the geometric [[logo]].

## Headers — small-caps emphasis style

- Section headers ("Programme Aims", "Learning Outcomes") set with **large initial cap + small caps**, white, on green-gradient header bars.
- The programme-name lockup uses the same small-caps treatment in blue ([[logo]]).
- Sub-headers and numbered sections (1, 2, 3) in **deep crimson red** ([[color-palette]]).

## Body — humanist/rounded sans

- Light-weight sans for paragraphs (white on colour panels, dark grey on white), justified or ranged-left; bold of the same family for inline emphasis ("**34.5** or above", "**Top 2%**").
- Bullets use a small colored arrow/wedge glyph (◥-like) — another echo of the triangle motif ([[graphic-devices]]).

## Chinese typography

- 跨學科組合學士課程 set in a rounded/heiti-style sans, green, within the lockup. No broader Chinese typographic system documented — gap for the guideline.

## Hierarchy observed in brochure

1. Page title: thin geometric caps, very large, white
2. Section bar: small-caps white on green gradient bar
3. Sub-head: bold red or bold body sans
4. Body: light sans ~9-10pt equivalent
5. Caption/credits: small grey ("Lyra, Year 1 | Study Focus: Hospitality + Psychology")
