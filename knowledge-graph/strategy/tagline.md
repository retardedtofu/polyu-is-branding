# Tagline

> ## BREAKING BARRIERS, EMPOWERING FUTURE LEADERS.

Used on the brochure cover (p.1) and all online banners. Always set in caps, three ranged lines, with a full stop.

## What it encodes

- **Breaking barriers** = the interdisciplinary promise — barriers between disciplines, faculties, and between classroom and world ([[brand-narrative]]).
- **Empowering future leaders** = the leadership outcome ([[brand-essence]]).

## The visual lockup (letter-replacement device)

Three letters are replaced by gradient geometric shapes drawn from the [[logo]] vocabulary:

| Letter | Shape | Gradient |
|---|---|---|
| **A** in BRE**A**KING | Triangle | orange → red/crimson |
| **O** in EMP**O**WERING | Circle | light green → green |
| **D** in LEA**D**ERS | Half-circle "D" | light blue → blue |

Remaining letters are a thin geometric sans ([[typography]]) in dark grey (~`#58595B`) on light backgrounds, or white reversed on gradient/photo backgrounds (brochure cover).

## Usage notes observed

- On banners the tagline appears with the sub-line: *"JS3000 BACHELOR'S DEGREE SCHEME IN INTERDISCIPLINARY STUDIES"* in letterspaced caps.
- Cover sets it white over the quadrant-grid graphic with the replacement shapes kept white/photographic — flexible treatment, needs codifying in [[brand-guideline-outline]].

## Gaps

- No Chinese version of the tagline found in supplied materials.
- No minimum-size or clear-space rule for the slogan lockup yet.
