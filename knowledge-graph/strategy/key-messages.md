# Key Messages

## Message house

**Roof ([[tagline]]):** *Breaking Barriers, Empowering Future Leaders.*

**Core message ([[brand-essence]]):** Nurturing future leaders to shape and sustain human-centric societies with rapidly evolving technology.

### Pillar 1 — Elite by design
- Highest entry requirement in PolyU ([[admissions]])
- Small cohort (~30), globally recruited ([[target-audience]])
- Direct support from PolyU senior management, incl. President and Vice Presidents (brochure p.7)
- *Proof:* [[cohort-2025-26]] Gaokao range 620–670

### Pillar 2 — Truly interdisciplinary
- Self-determined curriculum across all PolyU departments — "an upgrade of any PolyU programme" ([[positioning]])
- Three structured pathways so freedom never means drift ([[pathways]])
- Interdisciplinary Core spine: CUS101 → CUS202 → CUS201/301 → CUS401 capstone ([[curriculum]])
- *Proof:* 19 founding students, 19 distinct study plans ([[student-stories]])

### Pillar 3 — Radically personalised
- One-on-one **Personal Academic Mentor (PAM)**: PolyU's most accomplished scholars, incl. world's most-cited 2% scientists ([[mentorship-and-advising]])
- Four-tier advising structure; Dean-level attention to each student
- Personal focus named **in the degree title** ([[award-designations]])
- *Proof:* Lyra mentored by Prof. Kaye Chon; Yiran by Prof. Hongxia Yang ([[leadership-and-people]])

### Pillar 4 — A privileged student experience
- Guaranteed hall residence for first two years (others get one year, not guaranteed)
- Waived fees for human-skills workshops; enhanced overseas opportunities
- Safety net: can switch to a regular programme in Year 2 ([[student-experience-benefits]])
