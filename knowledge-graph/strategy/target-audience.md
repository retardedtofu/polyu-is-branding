# Target Audience

## Primary: top-performing school leavers

"Cream of the crop" students — the entry bar is explicitly the **highest in PolyU** ([[admissions]]):

| Segment | Route | Bar |
|---|---|---|
| Hong Kong local | JUPAS (JS3000) | HKDSE Best 5 ≥ **34.5** |
| Local non-JUPAS / International | IB | ≥ **40**/45 |
| International | GCE AL / IAL | ≥ **3A\* + 1A** |
| Mainland China | JEE (Gaokao) | **Top 2%** above Key University Cut-Off |

Plus strong English (e.g. HKDSE Level 5+). Recruitment is **global**, target intake ~**30 students** (deck slide 51).

## Psychographic profile (evidenced by [[cohort-2025-26]] and [[student-stories]])

- Ambitious, multi-interest students whose aspirations don't fit one box (AI + aviation, hospitality + psychology, maths + Spanish minor).
- Interest-driven self-starters comfortable designing their own path with guidance.
- Future-leader self-image: respond to prestige, access to senior people, and societal purpose.

## Secondary audiences the brand must also convince

- **Parents** — reassurance: prestige, guaranteed hall residence, a recognised award title (BA/BBA/BEng/BSc, see [[award-designations]]), option to switch back to a regular programme in Year 2.
- **School principals/counsellors** (JUPAS advice channel).
- **PolyU colleagues** — the analysed deck is itself an internal-persuasion artefact; faculty buy-in matters because mentors and subjects are drawn from all departments.
- **Mainland recruitment channels** — note the 小红书 (Xiaohongshu/RedNote) presence in [[digital-channels]].
