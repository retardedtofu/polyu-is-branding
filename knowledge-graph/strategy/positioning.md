# Positioning

## The claim

> **"The most prestigious programme tailored for top-performing students in PolyU."** (deck slides 7, 51; brochure: "PolyU's elite programme for top-performing students")

Two reinforcing positioning moves:

1. **Vertical (prestige):** highest entry score requirement in the whole of PolyU (deck slide 7). Small cohort (~30 students, global recruitment — slide 51; 19 in founding cohort, see [[cohort-2025-26]]). Mentors are PolyU's most accomplished scholars and senior management ([[mentorship-and-advising]]).
2. **Horizontal (scope):** *"BDSIS is an upgraded version of any PolyU programme"* (slide 9). It sits above the ~10 faculties/schools rather than beside them — any PolyU undergraduate offering can be assembled into a BDSIS self-determined curriculum ([[curriculum]]).

## Positioning statement (synthesised from sources)

For **top-performing students in Hong Kong, the Mainland and worldwide** who want more than a single-discipline degree, **BDSIS** is **PolyU's elite, interdisciplinary, fully personalised bachelor's scheme** that **upgrades any PolyU programme** with one-on-one mentorship from the university's most accomplished scholars — because **future leaders need both interdisciplinary mastery and deep human understanding** ([[brand-essence]]).

## Programme's unique features (verbatim, brochure p.3)

1. PolyU's elite programme for top-performing students
2. Interdisciplinary exposure and learning to tackle global challenges
3. Highly flexible learning pathways and self-determined curriculum
4. Personalised mentorship from PolyU's most accomplished scholars

## Brand architecture

PolyU (master brand) → College of Undergraduate Studies, CUS (endorser) → **BDSIS** (programme brand with own logo and tagline). Co-branding rules needed in the guideline — brochure footer pairs the PolyU and CUS logos beneath the BDSIS identity. See [[institutional-context]] and [[applications]].
