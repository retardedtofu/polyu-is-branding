# Institutional Context

## The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)

- Master brand; ~10 faculties/schools offering numerous undergraduate programmes (deck slide 9).
- PolyU logo (red 紅 monogram + bilingual wordmark) appears with BDSIS materials — brochure footer ribbon, cover.

## College of Undergraduate Studies (CUS)

- Host college of BDSIS; has its own logo (a colourful "CUS" mark, seen in brochure footer).
- Key people: Dean **Prof. Daniel Shek**; Associate Dean / BDSIS Programme Director **Prof. Alan Lau** ([[leadership-and-people]]).
- Functions: invites Personal Academic Mentors, runs the Undergraduate Studies Support (USS) team, hosts the CUS-coded core subjects (CUS101/201/202/301/401 — [[curriculum]]).
- Web home: www.polyu.edu.hk/cus/

## Co-branding observed (to codify in the guideline)

- Brochure cover: BDSIS logo block bottom-left; **deep-red gradient footer ribbon** carrying PolyU + CUS logos side by side.
- Hierarchy: BDSIS identity leads on programme collateral; PolyU/CUS endorse. Rules for clear space, order, and sizing between the three marks are **not yet documented** — flagged in [[brand-guideline-outline]].
