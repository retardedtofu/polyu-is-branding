# Curriculum

Three blocks (brochure p.5):

## 1 · Interdisciplinary Core (the BDSIS spine)

| Code | Subject | Credits | When | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| CUS101 | Introduction to Interdisciplinary Studies | 3 | Y1 S1 | Understand the world's problems and interdisciplinarity; develop a study plan |
| CUS202 | Interdisciplinary Research Methods | 3 | ~Y2/Y3 | Research design, methodology, ethics; prepares for capstone proposal and self-directed study |
| CUS201/301 | Reflections on Contemporary Issues I/II | 6 | Y2–Y3 | Student-led forums on local/national/regional/global issues with staff and external stakeholders; reflection assignments; transition "from knowing to acting" |
| CUS401 | Interdisciplinary Capstone | 6 | Y4 | Interdisciplinary project on an issue of significance, with **inter-sector partnership**; report + reflection |

(The deck sometimes labels these INT101/201/301/202/401 — earlier nomenclature; brochure's CUS codes are current.)

## 2 · Flexible Major — Self-determined Curriculum

- **Minimum 54 credits including the Interdisciplinary Core** (brochure); deck slide 26 shows 60 credits for the self-determined component.
- Built by the student via one of three [[pathways]], in consultation with the Programme Leader / Personal Academic Mentor ([[mentorship-and-advising]]).
- Students keep a **learning portfolio**, submitted with the capstone.
- Subjects may come from any PolyU department, and "a few from other universities" (Pathway C).

## 3 · Common UG Core

- **General University Requirements — 27 credits**: Language & Communication (LCR), Cluster-Area Requirements (CAR), Leadership Education and Development (LEAD), Service-Learning, AI & Data Analytics (AIDA), Innovation & Entrepreneurship (IE), Healthy Lifestyle (non-credit).
- **Internship** — 3–6 training credits, fulfils Work-Integrated Education (WIE); excluded from graduation credit count.
- **Free electives** — minimum 6 credits (deck shows 9–12 in worked examples).

## Key dates in a student's journey

Study focus indicated progressively: at admission (tentative) → by end of Y1 S1 → **confirmed by end of Semester 1 of Year 2** (deck slides 43, 45).
