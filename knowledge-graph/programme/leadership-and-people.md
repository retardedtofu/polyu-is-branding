# Leadership and People

The brand is fronted by named, high-credibility people — "faces of the brand".

## Programme leadership

### Prof. Daniel T.L. Shek
- Associate Vice President (Undergraduate Programme) & **Dean of the College of Undergraduate Studies (CUS)**
- Chair Professor of Applied Social Science
- Internationally renowned in Psychology, Social Work, Sociology, positive youth development, addiction and spirituality
- *Embodies the **human/society** half of the [[brand-essence]] vision*

### Prof. Alan Pak Tao Lau
- **Associate Dean of CUS & Programme Director, BDSIS** (deck presenter)
- Professor of Electrical and Electronic Engineering
- PhD Stanford (2008); Visiting Professor at Stanford (2019)
- Internationally renowned in optical communications, signal processing, ML applications in ICT, data centres, AI hardware infrastructure
- *Embodies the **technology** half of the vision*

> Note the deliberate pairing: a social scientist Dean + an engineer Associate Dean mirror the programme's two dimensions. Usable as a storytelling device.
