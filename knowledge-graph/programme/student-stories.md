# Student Stories

Real students function as brand personas and proof of the [[pathways]] in action.

## Lyra — Hospitality & Tourism Management + Psychology (Year 1)

> "I chose Hospitality Management and Psychology as my major areas of study because I believe this academic path allows me to go beyond the limits of a single discipline and become more competitive. By combining these two fields, I can gain a deeper understanding of consumer behaviour and human needs. I also feel truly supported by this programme, as my Personal Academic Mentor is Professor Kaye Chon, a leading scholar and Dean of the SHTM. He always shares his valuable life experiences with me, and recommends a wide range of activities that enrich my learning journey." (brochure p.7)

- Full 4-year worked study plan in deck slides 31–34 (HTM + APSS subjects around the CUS core).

## The 19 study plans (deck slides 40-42)

Founding-cohort breadth — every student a different combination, e.g.:

- Learning Science (Cognitive Psychology + Statistics + AI)
- AI + Aviation (low-altitude aerial vehicles/drones) → **BEng**
- Quantitative Finance + FinTech · Spatial Data Science & Smart Cities · Game Design
- Mathematical Modelling & Numerical Analysis **+ Minor in Spanish**
- Management + Minor in AI & Data Analytics → **BBA**

Award titles follow the *"Bachelor of X in Interdisciplinary Studies (with a focus on …)"* formula ([[award-designations]]).
