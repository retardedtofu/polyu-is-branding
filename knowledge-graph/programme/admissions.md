# Admissions

## Eligibility routes

Local • JUPAS | Local • Non-JUPAS | Non-Local • International | Non-Local • Mainland

## Minimum entrance requirements (brochure p.4)

| Qualification | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **HKDSE** (Local JUPAS, code JS3000) | Best 5 score ≥ **34.5**; all subjects carry the same weighting in admissions selection |
| **International Baccalaureate** | ≥ **40** / 45 |
| **GCE A-Level / International A-Level** | At least **3A\* + 1A** (other equivalent qualifications case-by-case) |
| **JEE / Gaokao** (Mainland) | **Top 2%** of candidates above the Key University Cut-Off Score |

Plus: PolyU General Entrance Requirements, and strong English proficiency expected (e.g. **HKDSE Level 5 or above**).

## Admissions-time commitments

- Students **tentatively propose an award designation at admission** ([[award-designations]], deck slide 55).
- Focus confirmed by end of Semester 1 of Year 2 ([[curriculum]]).

## Branding significance

The entry bar is itself a brand asset — "highest entry score requirement for whole PolyU" (deck slide 7) is the hardest proof of the elite [[positioning]]. Admissions stats should be rendered with the large-stat treatment in collateral (cf. bold **34.5**, **40**, **3A\*+1A**, **Top 2%** styling in the brochure).
