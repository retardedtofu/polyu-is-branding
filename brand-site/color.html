Proportion

The 80 / 15 / 5 rule

80 · ink & paper
15 · one family
5

80% neutral foundation · 15% a single spectrum family · 5% full-spectrum moments (logo, hairline, hero). This is the main shift from the launch collateral, where gradients covered whole pages: colour moves from wallpaper to signal.

Do

  • Let white space and ink carry the layout; deploy one gradient family per piece
  • Run gradients within a single hue family only
  • Use the spectrum hairline as the compact full-brand signature

Don't

  • Cover full pages in multi-family gradients
  • Invent cross-family gradients (purple→green etc.)
  • Use spectrum colours for long body text — text is Ink, Grey, or white reversed

⚠️ Values are sampled from the supplied RGB artwork. Confirm official CMYK / Pantone equivalents from the designer's .ai masters before print runs.
