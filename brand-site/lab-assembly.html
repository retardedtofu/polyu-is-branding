The Assembly

Clockwork choreography: every cell travels along the grid — one axis, a corner turn, home — rotating in 90° snaps as it goes. No free-floating drift; the motion obeys the same module the layouts do. Use as the website hero, a film opener, or in reverse as an outro.

Motion spec: Manhattan paths · 90° rotation snaps at each turn · 950 ms / cell · 80 ms stagger · cubic-bezier(0.6, 0, 0.12, 1) per leg · collective settle 240 ms · honours prefers-reduced-motion.
