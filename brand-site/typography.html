05 · Typography

Structure × voice

The type system encodes the brand core. A geometric grotesk speaks for technology and structure; a serif italic speaks for the human voice. The launch-era thin Futura-style caps are retired in favour of weights that hold their own at every size.

The pairing
Display & headings — Space Grotesk (500–700) · the technology register

Breaking barriers,
empowering future leaders.

Human voice — Source Serif 4 italic · quotes, stories, manifesto lines

"Judgement is the most valuable skill set — and judgement is a deep, personal understanding of human behaviour, needs and aspirations."

Body & UI — Inter (400–600)

Interdisciplinary studies offer students a comprehensive understanding of subjects from multiple perspectives, fostering the critical thinking and problem-solving skills needed to address challenges for future societies. Applicants are required to attain a HKDSE Best 5 score of 34.5 or above.

Fallbacks & office environments

All four faces are free (Google Fonts) — install organisation-wide. Where installation is impossible (e.g. shared PowerPoint): Trebuchet MS for headings, Calibri/Arial for body, Georgia Italic for the voice register, system Microsoft JhengHei for Chinese.

Do

  • Keep the serif italic exclusively for human voices — scarcity gives it meaning
  • Set numbers and codes (34.5, JS3000, CUS101) in the structural faces
  • Use the existing thin-caps artwork (tagline lockup) as supplied — it remains valid as artwork

Don't

  • Set new long headlines in ultra-thin weights — fragile at small sizes and low contrast
  • Mix a second grotesk or a decorative face into the system
  • Letter-space body text or set paragraphs in caps

Note: the original lockup/brochure typeface was never documented; this system supersedes it for new work while existing logo artwork stays untouched.
