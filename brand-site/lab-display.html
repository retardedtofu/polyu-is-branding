The Display

Shrink the cells and enlarge the grid, and the system turns into a low-resolution display, with each pixel a quarter circle that points the way the image flows. There are four programmes: a flow field, a marquee in a 5×7 cell font, the monogram blown up, and a live camera filter. Camera frames are processed in your browser; nothing is recorded or sent anywhere.

Programmes auto-cycle · 44 × 16 raster · camera stays local
