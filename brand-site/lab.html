Appendix · The Lab

The system,
in motion

Eight interactive pieces made from the Cell System: motion studies, production tools, and campaign pieces. Each one is its own page.

EXHIBIT 1 · MOTION

The Display

The grid as a low-res screen: a flow field, a marquee, the monogram, and a live camera filter.
EXHIBIT 2 · MOTION

The Kaleidoscope

Seventy-two discs turning in step. An ambient pattern for screens and receptions.
EXHIBIT 3 · TOOL

The Composer

Stamp, rotate, and export. Production-ready SVG or PNG from the grid.
EXHIBIT 4 · TOOL

Your Mark

Turn any text into a cell mark, on a grid up to 256×256. The same text always gives the same mark.
EXHIBIT 5 · CAMPAIGN

The Open Seat

The founding cohort as a grid: nineteen seats taken, eleven open.
EXHIBIT 6 · CAMPAIGN

The Toy Bin

Drop the shapes in, then snap them to the grid. Real physics.
EXHIBIT 7 · TOOL

The Cell Code

A scannable mark. The shape and rotation carry the data; the colour stays on brand. Encode one, then read it back with the camera.
